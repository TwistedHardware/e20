WORLD CLASS EVENT MANAGEMENT

E20 has a number of experienced and credible individuals who have worked on major world events such as the G20, APEC, Commonwealth Heads of Government meetings, as well as a number corporate events across the world.

We can deliver you a successful, dignified and safe event. We strive to deliver the best possible experience for the client and an approach to event planning that ensures absolute precision and harmony when you need it most.

We have a team of dedicated and experienced professionals who have led major events in Australia as part of the public service, law enforcement and intelligence communities available now to assist with the following:

  • Provide ‘health checks’ on every aspect of event planning for your events to ensure that the arrangements reflects world’s best practice and
  • Provide advice and staff supplementation in dealing with airport operations, accommodation solutions, customs, immigration and quarantine, accreditation and credential management, concierge services, interpretation services, transport, media and broadcasting services.
  • Provide advice and arrange locations, venues and providers for additional business or social events across Australia that can be tailored for your needs.
  • Providing security and public safety advice and risks assessments of to ensure that your event is safe with appropriate security measures in place.
  • General advice on participating in and conducting events.

At the completion of the event we also provide “lessons learnt” service so that knowledge transfer can be used for corporate and staff development services.

E20 can provide advice on other destinations and venues for smaller delegations or business events.
